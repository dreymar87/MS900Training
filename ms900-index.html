Recommended Study Path
1
Read Domain 1 — Cloud Concepts (5–10%). Shortest domain, essential foundation. Check off each skill card as you read it.
2
Read Domain 3 — Security & Compliance (25–30%). Do this before D2 — understanding identity and security makes the app layer in D2 click much faster.
3
Read Domain 2 — M365 Apps & Services (45–50%). Largest domain. Use the in-page jump bar to navigate between the 5 module sections. Pay close attention to the ⚠️ confusion callouts.
4
Read Domain 4 — Pricing & Licensing (10–15%). Quickest read. Then follow the "What's Next" study path at the bottom of the page.
5
Take the Practice Quiz. Aim for 80%+ before moving on. Use your wrong answers to identify which domain pages to re-read.
6
Skim the Glossary — focus on the ⭐ high-priority terms. These are the highest-frequency exam concepts. Good for the night before.
7
Take Microsoft's official free practice assessment (50 questions, real exam format). If you score 700+, book the exam. If not, revisit weak areas.
